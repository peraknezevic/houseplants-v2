Peperomia is the 2nd largest genus of the family Piperaceae with 1373 accepted species. They are found in tropical and subtropical regions, most are native to Central and South America.

Listed bellow are the varieties that are being cultivated around the world.

If there's a plant that's missing here, or you would like to send a photo for the variety that still doesn't have one, or some information isn't correct, please contact me via e-mail at houseplants.xyz@gmail.com or via instagram @houseplants.xyz
